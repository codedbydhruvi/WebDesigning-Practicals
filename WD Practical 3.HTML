Healthy Lifestyle

A healthy lifestyle means taking care of both body and mind. Regular exercise, balanced diet, and proper sleep help us live a better life.

Maintaining good health improves energy, focus, and overall well-being.

Steps to Maintain a Healthy Life

  1. Wake up early
  2. Exercise regularly
  3. Eat balanced meals
  4. Stay hydrated
  5. Sleep at least 7–8 hours

Healthy Foods

  • Fruits
  • Vegetables
  • Nuts
  • Whole grains

Types of Exercises

Yoga
Improves flexibility and mental peace.
Running
Strengthens heart and increases stamina.
Strength Training
Builds muscles and improves body strength.
